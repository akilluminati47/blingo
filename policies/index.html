Privacy Policy — the short version

Effective 21 Jul 2026

  • We don't collect anything. No accounts, no ads, no analytics, no tracking cookies. There's no server logging your play.
  • Your settings and progress (audio levels, your prestige streak & best time, and a random multiplayer id) are saved only in your browser's localStorage, on your device — never sent to us.
  • Multiplayer is peer-to-peer (PeerJS). To connect a lobby, a code is registered on the public PeerJS broker and your browser then exchanges connection info and live gameplay data directly with the other players — which, like any direct connection, means those peers can see your IP address. We run no game server and store none of it.
  • This page loads a font and the PeerJS library from third-party CDNs, which see your IP as part of serving those files. That's the extent of it.
  • Because we hold no data, there's nothing for us to export or delete — clearing your browser storage wipes everything Blingo ever saved.
